Power System Lab at IITB

A leading institution focused on advanced research and practical applications in power systems, power electronics, and microprocessor control systems.

Power System

Delve into the complexities of power distribution, generation, and transmission, and explore how these systems maintain energy flow across vast networks.

Power Electronics

Study the innovative electronics used in transforming, controlling, and regulating electrical power with high efficiency and minimal loss.

Microprocessor Controller

Gain hands-on experience with microprocessors and how they are integral in optimizing performance and control of power systems and various devices.
